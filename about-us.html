About Us

Future Fixit Industries Private Limited is considered as one of the leading suppliers of construction products in India. Future Fixit Industries Private Limited is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company. Future Fixit’s fastest growing Indian company in the field of construction product with the brand name of Future Fixit. Our product range includes construction products such as epoxy grout, adhesives, admixtures, and other formulations for applications like grouting, fixing tiles, stones, waterproofing, etc.

Cooperation on an equal balance: transparency, mutual trust, and fairness define the business relationship between Future Fixit and its partners. In the spirit of the Chairman, these values are at the core of every company's decision and every action. For mutual success today and in the future.

Founder & CEO

Vasudev Patidar

Future Fixit Industries Private Limited is a leading manufacturer of advanced adhesives, grouts, cements, wall putty, waterproofing chemicals, and tile and stone cleaners in India. The company was founded by Vasudev Pandey with a vision to redefine the modern Indian industrial revolution. Future Fixit’s commitment to innovation, research, and development has led to the creation of truly unique products with exceptional global impact.

Our Vision & Mission

VISION

Future Fixit is built on a clear vision: to be the best adhesives company in India. Our vision, combined with our mission & beliefs, ground us and set the cornerstone for employee and company success.

Future Fixit wants to contribute a deeper understanding of the Tile and Stone market to reflect on its customers by its products to "Create strength for Future."

MISSION

We are inspired by the desire to develop products that "Create strength for Future", "Beam enthusiasm", improve quality of life, and help safeguard natural resources. Our mission revelation reflects this. It outlines our values, strengths, and strategies.

Our mission is based on customer and people-centric values, which shape our corporate culture, ranging from a focus on the future and earnings to cultural diversity. Sustainability and Responsibility are part of this set of values and thus of our actions.
