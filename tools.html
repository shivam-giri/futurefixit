NOTCH TROWEL

Made from high-quality steel, this trowel offers different notch sizes to control the thickness of adhesive. It is perfect for tile and stone adhesive applications, providing a consistent finish on all surfaces. Suitable for both wall and floor applications.

  • Material: Premium Stainless Steel
  • Plate Thickness: 2 mm
  • Notch Sizes: 3 mm (Glass Mosaic Fixing), 6 mm & 10 mm
  • Packaging: 1 Piece
  • Color: Silver

GROUTING TROWEL (RUBBER FLOAT)

Crafted from high-quality imported rubber with a high-grip handle and flat bottom, this grouting trowel is designed to fill tile and stone joints perfectly. It works effectively with both cement-based and epoxy-based grout materials, ideal for grouting on both floors and walls.

  • Material: High Density Flexible Rubber
  • Packaging: 1 Piece
  • Color: White or Green

CLEANING SPONGE

Imported from Germany, this high-quality cellulose and fiber cleaning sponge is designed with a square shape for a firm grip. Perfect for cleaning grout after application, it retains enough water to smooth and level joints. Suitable for use on all surfaces.

  • Material: High Density Cellulose Sponge
  • Sponge Thickness: 2 mm
  • Size: 3 mm (Notch for Glass Mosaic Fixing), 6 mm & 10 mm
  • Packaging: 1 Piece
  • Color: Yellow
